About me

As a child, I have fallen in love with games and spent a lot of time enjoying them. Bit by bit, I started to admire those, who create such entertaining worlds and decided to become one of them. That is how I started coding and became obsessed with the idea of using my programming skills to create games and apps others would love. In 2015 I first started tinkering with Unity and, later on, Unreal Engine 4. By that moment I was already proficient in C# and C++. As a Unity Developer, I managed to further improve my programming and solution solving skills, as well as acquire solid communication and management skill. I got proficient in the development of mobile and desktop games, setting up client-server communication, and usage of different profiling tools. What is more, I had experience leading a small project.
